Chicken Alfredo

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken: Sliced chicken breast, seasoned and cooked to perfection.
  • Pasta: Fettuccine or any long pasta of choice.
  • Butter: Melted butter for a rich base.
  • Cream: Heavy cream for a creamy, velvety sauce.
  • Cheese: Freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Garlic: Minced garlic to enhance flavor.
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, and optional parsley for garnish.

Steps

    1. Season and cook chicken until golden brown; set aside.
    2. Boil pasta until al dente; drain and set aside.
    3. Saut√© garlic in butter, then add heavy cream and simmer.
    4. Stir in Parmesan cheese until the sauce thickens.
    5. Toss pasta in the sauce, add chicken, and serve warm.
