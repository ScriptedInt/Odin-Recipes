Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Ingredients

  • Pasta: Use high-quality spaghetti.
  • Meat: Pancetta or guanciale for the perfect savory flavor.
  • Eggs: Fresh eggs form the creamy base of the sauce.
  • Cheese: Grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese for a sharp, nutty flavor.
  • Garlic: A clove of garlic lightly fried for aroma.
  • Black Pepper: Freshly ground black pepper to add a hint of spice.

Steps

    1. Cook the spaghetti until al dente.
    2. Fry the pancetta or guanciale until crispy.
    3. Whisk together eggs, grated cheese, and black pepper.
    4. Drain pasta and quickly mix it with the egg mixture and pancetta.
    5. Serve immediately with additional cheese and pepper.
